Let's try to answer your tax questions!

Our team is here to support you. Please see our FAQ or submit a question via chat and we will get back to you!

We can not answer:

  • Questions about taxes filed with a different service (H&R Block, TurboTax)
  • The amount of refund you will receive

GetYourRefund.org is a non-profit service built by Code for America in partnership with IRS-certified Volunteer Income Tax Assistance (VITA) sites nationally.
