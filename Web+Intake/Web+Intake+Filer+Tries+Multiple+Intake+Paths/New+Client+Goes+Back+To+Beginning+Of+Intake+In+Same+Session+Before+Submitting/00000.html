Great! Let's help you collect your stimulus.

Heads up: In order to claim your stimulus by the deadline, we need all required documents by October 8th. You should get the payment automatically and do not need to fill out this form if you received:

  • Social Security retirement, survivor, or disability benefits (SSDI)
  • Railroad Retirement benefits
  • Supplemental Security Income (SSI)
  • VA Compensation and Pension (C&P)

Need additional assistance?

The United Way's helpline is available to answer your questions and can help you complete this form over the phone.
